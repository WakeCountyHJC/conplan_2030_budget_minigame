Participatory Budgeting: Raleigh's Consolidated Plan 2026–2030

In this game, you can describe how you would distribute the funds allocated by the City of Raleigh to the 2026–2030 Consolidated Plan. We present the five funding “buckets” defined from their literature and challenge you to find a fair allocation of funds among them.

We believe that residents have the right to participate in the budgeting decisions made by their municipality. If you are interested in our work, we would love to hear from you and keep you in touch with coming campaigns and events.
